Police Emergency and Important Numbers

Service Number Description
Police Emergency (General) 100 For all general police emergencies.
Child Helpline 1098 For reporting child abuse, neglect, or children in distress.
Women Helpline 1091 For reporting domestic violence, harassment, or other issues related to women's safety.
Cyber Crime Helpline 1930 For reporting online fraud, cyberbullying, and other cyber crimes.
Senior Citizen Helpline 1090 For assistance and reporting issues related to senior citizens.
Ambulance 108 Emergency ambulance services.
Fire Brigade 101 Emergency fire services.
Traffic Police Helpline 1073 For traffic related issues.
Missing Persons Helpline 1094 For reporting missing persons.

Note: These numbers are general emergency numbers and may vary slightly depending on your location. Please check with your local police department for the most accurate and up-to-date information.
